ABOUT ME

NEW YORK

I majored in English during undergraduate. Language learning and linguistics are fun. What is the most valuable part about this experience is that it inspired me to pay attention to how people learn (in a general way) and think about how to facilitate this process.

I have a Master’s degree in Learning Technology and Experience Design at New York University. I have very valuable opportunities to do internships and collaborate with real clients, which honed my UX skills. I also tried to do some front-end code at that time. It turns out I love it. I’m still continuously learning!

Personally, I have a passion for nature and enjoy exploring the world. I care about people’s wellbeing and would like to bring a bit of empathy, fun and joy to the world. Hopefully I promote these ideas in my designs.
